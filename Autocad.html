Course Overview

What You'll Learn

Master AutoCad from basics to advanced techniques. Learn 2D drafting, 3D modeling, rendering, annotation, dimensioning, plotting, and customization. Work on real-world projects including architectural plans, mechanical drawings, and interior designs.

Who This Course Is For

Architects, engineers, interior designers, drafters, students, and anyone interested in CAD design. Beginners with no prior AutoCad experience as well as professionals looking to upgrade their skills are welcome.

Course Format

Hands-on training with project-based learning. Includes video tutorials, practical exercises, real-world projects, and one-on-one mentoring. Access to AutoCad software and templates included during the course.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to purchase AutoCAD software for this course?
No, we provide educational licenses of AutoCAD to all enrolled students for the duration of the course. You'll also learn how to obtain free educational versions for continued practice after the course.
What are the system requirements for running AutoCAD?
Minimum requirements: Windows 10 64-bit, 8GB RAM, 10GB free disk space, and a dedicated graphics card with 2GB VRAM. For optimal performance, we recommend 16GB RAM, SSD storage, and a graphics card with 4GB+ VRAM.
Is this course suitable for Mac users?
Yes, AutoCAD is available for Mac, though the interface differs slightly from the Windows version. We provide guidance for both platforms, but most professional environments use Windows, so we recommend Windows if possible.
How much drawing ability do I need for this course?
No traditional drawing skills are required. AutoCAD is about technical precision rather than freehand drawing. However, basic spatial visualization skills and attention to detail are helpful.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, upon successful completion of all modules and final project, you'll receive a Professional Certification in AutoCAD 2D/3D from Coded Mind Technology Incubator. This certificate is recognized by local industry employers.
