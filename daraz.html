What You'll Learn

From setting up your seller account to advanced marketing techniques, this course covers everything you need to succeed on Daraz. Learn product listing optimization, order management, customer service excellence, and data analysis.

Aspiring entrepreneurs, existing business owners, marketing professionals, and anyone interested in starting or expanding their e-commerce business on the Daraz platform.

Self-paced online learning with video lessons, practical assignments, downloadable resources, and live Q&A sessions. Accessible on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
