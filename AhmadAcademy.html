Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior knowledge to start?
No prior knowledge is required. We have courses designed for complete beginners as well as advanced learners. Our teachers will assess your level and place you in the appropriate class.
What equipment do I need for online classes?
You'll need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet connection. We'll guide you through installing any necessary software for the classes. Most classes are conducted via Zoom or similar platforms.
How are the classes scheduled?
We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate students from different time zones. You can choose class timings that work best for you, and we'll match you with a teacher available during those times.
What age groups do you teach?
We teach students of all ages, from young children (starting at age 5) to adults. Our teaching methods are tailored to each age group to ensure effective learning.
Do you offer trial classes?
Yes, we offer a free trial class so you can experience our teaching methodology and meet your potential teacher before committing to the course.
