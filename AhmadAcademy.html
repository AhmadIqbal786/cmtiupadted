Course Overview

What You'll Learn

From Arabic alphabet recognition to fluent Quran reading with proper Tajweed. Master pronunciation, articulation points, and the rules of recitation to read Quran as it was revealed.

Who This Course Is For

Absolute beginners with no prior Arabic knowledge, children and adults wanting to learn Quran reading, and those looking to improve their existing recitation skills with proper Tajweed.

Course Format

One-on-one online sessions with qualified teachers, flexible scheduling, personalized learning plans, and continuous assessment. Accessible on all devices with interactive learning materials.

Course Modules

Module 1: Arabic Alphabet & Basics
  • Introduction to Arabic letters and their forms
  • Proper pronunciation of each letter
  • Short vowels (Harakat) - Fatha, Kasra, Damma
  • Letter connection and word formation
  • Reading practice with simple words
Module 2: Quranic Reading Foundation
  • Long vowels (Madd letters)
  • Tanween (Nunation)
  • Shaddah and Sukoon
  • Reading complete verses
  • Common Quranic words and phrases
Module 3: Tajweed Rules Introduction
  • Rules of Noon Sakinah and Tanween
  • Rules of Meem Sakinah
  • Qalqalah letters and their application
  • Madd (elongation) rules
  • Practical application in Quranic verses
Module 4: Advanced Tajweed
  • Rules of Raa (ر)
  • Rules of Laam (ل)
  • Heavy and light letters
  • Stopping rules (Waqf)
  • Advanced madd rules
Module 5: Fluency & Practice
  • Reading longer verses fluently
  • Recitation of complete Surahs
  • Speed and accuracy development
  • Common mistakes and corrections
  • Final assessment and certification

About The Instructor

Sheikh Ahmad Al-Misri

With over 15 years of experience in teaching Quran and Tajweed, Sheikh Ahmad holds Ijazah (certification) in Quran recitation from Al-Azhar University, Egypt. He has taught hundreds of students worldwide and specializes in making Quran learning accessible to non-Arabic speakers.

Sheikh Ahmad's teaching methodology focuses on building strong foundations while making the learning process enjoyable and spiritually rewarding. His patience and dedication have helped countless students achieve their goal of reading Quran fluently.

He believes that every Muslim should have the opportunity to connect with the Quran through proper recitation, and his teaching approach combines traditional methods with modern educational techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior Arabic knowledge?
No prior knowledge is required. This course is designed for absolute beginners. We start from the Arabic alphabet and build up gradually to fluent Quran reading.
What equipment do I need for the classes?
You'll need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet connection. We use Zoom or similar platforms for classes. All learning materials will be provided digitally.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, you will receive a verified certificate of completion that acknowledges your achievement in Quran reading. The certificate can be shared and included in your records.
How long will it take to complete the course?
The course duration depends on your learning pace and schedule. On average, students complete it in 6-9 months with 2-3 classes per week. We offer flexible pacing to suit your needs.
Do you offer trial classes?
Yes, we offer a free trial class so you can experience our teaching methodology, meet your potential teacher, and assess if our program is the right fit for you before committing.
