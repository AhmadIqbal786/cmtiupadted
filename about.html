At Coded Mind Technology Incubator, we're dedicated to bridging the gap between ambition and expertise in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

Our Story

Founded in Katlang, Mardan, Coded Mind Technology Incubator began with a simple vision: to create a center of excellence where aspiring technology professionals could gain practical, industry-relevant skills.

What started as a small training center has grown into a comprehensive technology incubator, offering a wide range of courses from basic IT skills to advanced specializations like AI and cybersecurity. Our journey has been shaped by the success stories of our students, many of whom have gone on to build rewarding careers in technology.

Today, we continue to evolve our curriculum to meet the demands of the digital economy, ensuring our students are always learning the most current and valuable skills.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals with cutting-edge technological skills through quality education, hands-on training, and mentorship. We strive to make technology education accessible and relevant, creating pathways to successful careers in the digital world.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology incubator in the region, recognized for producing highly skilled professionals who drive innovation and digital transformation across industries. We envision a future where our graduates are at the forefront of technological advancement.
