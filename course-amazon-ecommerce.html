Course Overview

What You'll Learn

From Amazon FBA fundamentals to advanced scaling strategies, this course covers everything you need to become proficient in Amazon e-commerce. Learn product research, listing optimization, PPC advertising, inventory management, and international expansion.

Who This Course Is For

Aspiring Amazon sellers, existing sellers looking to scale, e-commerce professionals, entrepreneurs, and marketing professionals interested in expanding their e-commerce skills. No prior Amazon experience required.

Course Format

Self-paced online learning with video lessons, practical exercises, real-world projects, and live Q&A sessions. Accessible on all devices with dedicated support from Amazon e-commerce experts.

Course Modules

Module 1: Amazon Marketplace Fundamentals
  • Introduction to Amazon FBA and business models
  • Setting up your Amazon seller account
  • Understanding Amazon's fee structure
  • Navigating Seller Central dashboard
  • Amazon policies and compliance requirements
Module 2: Product Research & Selection
  • Niche selection strategies
  • Product research techniques and tools
  • Competitor analysis
  • Profitability calculations
  • Sourcing products from manufacturers
Module 3: Listing Optimization
  • Keyword research for Amazon
  • Writing compelling product titles
  • Creating persuasive product descriptions
  • Optimizing images and A+ content
  • Pricing strategies for maximum conversion
Module 4: Amazon PPC Advertising
  • Understanding Amazon advertising ecosystem
  • Setting up Sponsored Products campaigns
  • Sponsored Brands and Display ads
  • Keyword targeting strategies
  • Optimizing campaigns for profitability
Module 5: Inventory & Supply Chain Management
  • Inventory planning and forecasting
  • Shipping to Amazon fulfillment centers
  • Managing inventory health
  • Working with freight forwarders
  • Avoiding long-term storage fees
Module 6: Scaling & International Expansion
  • Brand registry and protection
  • Expanding to European marketplaces
  • Managing multiple product lines
  • Hiring virtual assistants
  • Exit strategies and business valuation

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior e-commerce experience for this course?
No, this course is designed for both beginners and experienced sellers. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced strategies, ensuring everyone can follow along regardless of their prior experience.
How much investment is required to start an Amazon FBA business?
While the initial investment can vary, we recommend having at least PKR 100,000-200,000 to start with product sourcing, shipping, and initial advertising. However, we also cover strategies for starting with a smaller budget and scaling gradually.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, you will receive a verified certificate of completion that you can share on LinkedIn and include in your resume. The certificate demonstrates your proficiency in Amazon e-commerce strategies and practical applications.
How long will it take to complete the course?
The course includes approximately 40 hours of video content and another 20-30 hours of exercises and projects. Most students complete it in 6-8 weeks studying part-time, but you can learn at your own pace.
Is there any hands-on project work?
Yes! The course includes several practical projects including creating a complete Amazon business plan, conducting product research, optimizing product listings, setting up PPC campaigns, and developing an international expansion strategy.
