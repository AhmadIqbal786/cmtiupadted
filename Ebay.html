Course Curriculum

Our structured curriculum takes you from beginner to expert through 6 comprehensive modules.

Module 1: eBay Fundamentals

Understanding the eBay ecosystem, seller levels, fees, and policies.

Module 2: Account Setup & Optimization

Creating your seller account, setting up payment methods, and optimizing your store.

Module 3: Product Sourcing
