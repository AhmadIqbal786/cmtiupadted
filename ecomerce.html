Course Curriculum

Our structured curriculum takes you from beginner to expert through 8 comprehensive modules.

Module 1: E-commerce Fundamentals

Understanding the e-commerce landscape, business models, and legal requirements.

Module 2: Platform Selection

Comparing Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce and others to find the right fit.

Module 3: Product Sourcing

Working with suppliers, dropshipping, inventory management, and fulfillment.

Module 4: Store Design & UX

Creating high-converting store layouts, product pages, and checkout flows.

Module 5: Digital Marketing

Facebook/Instagram ads, Google Ads, influencer marketing, and content strategy.

Module 6: Email & Retention Marketing

Building email lists, segmentation, automation, and customer retention strategies.

Module 7: Analytics & Optimization

Tracking key metrics, A/B testing, and conversion rate optimization techniques.

Module 8: Scaling Your Business

Advanced strategies for scaling your store, outsourcing, and building a brand.

You'll have lifetime access to all course materials, including any future updates we make to the content.

No prior experience is needed. The course is designed for complete beginners but also includes advanced strategies for those with some e-commerce experience.
