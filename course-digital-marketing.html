Course Overview

What You'll Learn

From digital marketing fundamentals to advanced campaign strategies, this course covers everything you need to become proficient in digital marketing. Learn SEO, social media marketing, content strategy, PPC advertising, email marketing, analytics, and conversion optimization.

Who This Course Is For

Marketing professionals, business owners, students, freelancers, and anyone interested in understanding and leveraging digital marketing. No prior marketing experience required, but basic computer skills are helpful.

Course Format

Hybrid learning with video lessons, practical exercises, real-world projects, and live Q&A sessions. Accessible on all devices with dedicated support from digital marketing experts.

  • Customer journey mapping
  • Developing a digital marketing strategy
  • Digital marketing ethics and regulations
Module 2: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • How search engines work
  • Keyword research and analysis

  • Google Ads campaign setup

  • Google Analytics implementation

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need marketing experience for this course?
No prior marketing experience is required. The course starts with digital marketing fundamentals and gradually builds up to advanced concepts. However, basic computer skills and familiarity with social media platforms will be helpful.
What tools will I need?
You'll need a computer with internet access. We'll guide you through setting up free accounts for tools like Google Analytics, Google Ads, and various social media platforms. All software used in the course is either free or we provide access during the course.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, you will receive a verified certificate of completion that you can share on LinkedIn and include in your resume. The certificate demonstrates your proficiency in digital marketing strategies and tools.
How long will it take to complete the course?
The course is designed to be completed in 3 months with part-time study. It includes approximately 50 hours of video content and another 30-40 hours of exercises and projects. However, you can learn at your own pace and have access to course materials for 12 months.
Is there any hands-on project work?
Yes! The course includes several practical projects including creating a complete digital marketing strategy, running a small-scale PPC campaign, performing SEO analysis on a website, and developing a content marketing plan for a business.
