Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to take this course?
No prior experience is required. This course is designed for complete beginners as well as experienced entrepreneurs looking to expand their e-commerce knowledge. We start with the fundamentals and progress to advanced strategies.
How long will it take to complete the course?
The course includes over 300 hours of content, but the time to complete it depends on your pace. Most students complete the core modules within 4-6 weeks studying part-time. You'll have lifetime access, so you can learn at your own pace.
Will I get support during the course?
Yes! You'll have access to our exclusive student community where you can ask questions and get feedback. We also hold weekly Q&A sessions with the instructor and teaching assistants.
What if I don't see results after taking the course?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you complete the course modules, implement the strategies, and don't see value in what you've learned, we'll refund your investment.
Do you cover specific e-commerce platforms?
Yes, we cover all major platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Amazon, and eBay. The strategies taught are applicable to any platform, and we provide platform-specific tutorials for the most popular options.
