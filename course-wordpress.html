Course Overview

What You'll Learn

From basic site setup to advanced development techniques, this course covers everything you need to build custom WordPress solutions. Learn theme development, plugin creation, customization techniques, and how to optimize WordPress sites for performance and security.

Who This Course Is For

Aspiring web developers, bloggers and content creators, small business owners, marketing professionals, freelancers looking to expand their service offerings, and anyone interested in website development.

Course Format

Hybrid learning with video lessons, coding exercises, real-world projects, and live Q&A sessions. Accessible on all devices with dedicated support from WordPress experts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience for this course?
No prior coding experience is required for the first modules. The course starts with WordPress fundamentals and gradually progresses to more advanced development topics. However, having basic HTML and CSS knowledge will be helpful for the theme development modules.
What software will I need for this course?
You'll need a computer with internet access and a code editor (we recommend Visual Studio Code). We'll guide you through setting up a local development environment using software like XAMPP or Local by Flywheel, which are free to use.
Will I be able to build a complete website after this course?
Yes! By the end of the course, you'll have built multiple websites including a portfolio site, a business website, and an e-commerce store. You'll also create custom themes and plugins, giving you the skills to build virtually any type of website with WordPress.
How much time should I dedicate to this course each week?
We recommend dedicating 6-8 hours per week to complete the course in 2 months. This includes watching video lessons, completing exercises, and working on projects. The course is designed to be flexible, so you can adjust the pace according to your schedule.
What kind of support will I receive during the course?
You'll have access to our dedicated support team and instructor through our online platform. We also host weekly Q&A sessions where you can get your questions answered directly. Additionally, you'll join a community of fellow students where you can collaborate and share knowledge.
