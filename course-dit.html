Diploma in Information Technology

The DIT course provides a comprehensive foundation in IT. Learn computer basics, networking, programming, software applications, and web technologies to prepare for a successful career in technology.

Enroll Now

Course Overview

What You'll Learn

From computer fundamentals to advanced programming concepts, this course covers everything you need to become proficient in information technology. Learn computer systems, office productivity, programming, web technologies, networking, and database management.

Who This Course Is For

High school graduates, office workers, career changers, small business owners, and anyone interested in understanding technology fundamentals. No prior IT experience required.

Course Format

Hybrid learning with video lessons, practical exercises, real-world projects, and live Q&A sessions. Accessible on all devices with dedicated support from IT experts.

Program Structure

Diploma in Information Technology (DIT) is a one year course comprised of two terms duration of six months each. It is totally based on computer skills and knowledge. Major part of this course is practical based.

DIT Examinations

The DIT examinations are conducted by the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Board of Technical Education, Peshawar twice in a year called First-Term exam and Second-Term Exam in the months of February and August respectively.

Admissions

Admissions in DIT program are offered twice in a year in the months of January and August. Admission forms can be received from and submitted to the office in duty hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior IT experience for this course?
No prior IT experience is required. The course starts with computer fundamentals and gradually builds up to more advanced concepts. Basic familiarity with using a computer is helpful but not mandatory.
What software will I need for this course?
You'll need a computer with internet access. We'll guide you through installing necessary software including Microsoft Office, Python, code editors, and other free tools. All software used in the course is either free or we provide access during the course.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, you will receive a verified diploma certificate of completion that you can share on LinkedIn and include in your resume. The certificate demonstrates your proficiency in information technology fundamentals.
How long will it take to complete the course?
The course is designed to be completed in 6 months with part-time study. It includes approximately 80 hours of video content and another 50-60 hours of exercises and projects. However, you can learn at your own pace and have access to course materials for 12 months.
Is there any hands-on project work?
Yes! The course includes several practical projects including building a complete computer system setup guide, creating a business presentation, developing a simple website, writing Python programs, and designing a basic database.
